Call for Papers

Microservices are a paradigm for software architecture: small services in separated processes take the place of large applications. This way monolithic architecture can be avoided, and systems are easily scalable and changeable. This approach has deep consequences: very different architectures are needed, many different technology stacks might be used in one project, and deployment as well as operations of these services leads to many new challenges. The microXchg conference looks at all these aspects of Microservices.

Possible subjects for talks include:

  • Experiences with Microservices
  • Microservices-based Architectures
  • Operations of Microservices
  • Technologies for the implementation, deployment and operation of Microservices
  • Migration of monoliths to Microservices
  • Organizational consequences of Microservices

The conferences should enable discussions and knowledge exchange. Therefore moderated sessions such as Fish Bowls or Open Spaces can also be submitted. The talks will be in english language.

The Call for Papers will end at 1st November 2015. We are looking forward to your submissions!
